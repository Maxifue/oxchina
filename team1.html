Baichuan Liu

Baichuan is a second year student reading Geography at St Anne’s College, University of Oxford. Baichuan have studied and lived in several cities around the world, ranging from Beijing, Cairo to Oxford. At Oxford, his interest lies in post-colonialism in the 21st century and Middle East geopolitics, namely the Israeli-Palestinian conflict. Aside from his academic pursuit, Baichuan is passionate about exploring the world through the lens of photography, namely countries such as Israel and Iran.

Zhuoyu Shen

Ella Shen is a final year undergraduate from Pembroke College studying Mathematics and Philosophy. Prior to Oxford, she lived in Hangzhou and Singapore. She enjoys understanding how things work, which took her from contemplating the structure of reality to initiating discussions on modern feminism in OCF2019. She worked at a castle in the Czech Republic and two education startups to travel and connect with people. In her free time, she swims, runs, analyses movies and posts on Instagram.

Chengkai Xie

Chengkai Xie is a first-year student reading Philosophy, Politics and Economics at Brasenose College. Born in Qingdao, the city’s rich history and vast ocean have brought him up with passion and ambition. Having lived in the UK for five years, Chengkai’s interests in politics motivate him to work in the frontline of politics, representing the voice of the UK-Chinese community. He is also a committee member of Oxford University Conservative Association (OUCA).
